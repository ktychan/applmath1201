\section{Scaling Models}
\begin{frame}
  \frametitle{Log-log transformation}

  The \hlmain{log-log transformation} of \(y = c x^{\alpha}\) is
  \[
    \ln(y) = \alpha \ln(x) + \ln(c).
  \]

  Log-log transformations are ``undoable'' by raising both sides to base \(e\).
\end{frame}
